Why We Built This

INDIA'S
FARMING
CRISIS

India is one of the largest agricultural nations in the world. Farming contributes 16% of India's GDP and more than 50% of our population depends on it. Yet farmers still face labour shortages, unpredictable climate, and growing workload.

This inspired us to combine Technology + Agriculture to make farming smarter, more accessible, and sustainable for every Indian farmer.

Multi-Purpose Robot

WHAT IT
CAN DO

AgroTaskMaster is a complete agricultural assistant — one robot, infinite possibilities.

🚜
01
Ploughing
Automated soil preparation with precision depth control, reducing the need for heavy manual labor and bullock-driven equipment.
🌱
02
Seed Dispensing
Controlled seed placement with adjustable spacing and depth — ensuring optimal crop yield from every field.
📐
03
Levelling
Smooths uneven terrain for uniform water distribution and improved crop growth across the entire field.
💧
04
Watering
Smart irrigation attachment with adjustable flow rates — waters crops evenly and conserves water resources efficiently.
✂️
05
Grass Cutting & Harvesting
Precision cutting blades for weed removal and crop harvesting — saving hours of manual labour per acre.
📷
06
Scarecrow + HD Camera
24/7 crop surveillance with HD camera and intelligent scarecrow function — protecting crops from birds and intruders.
🔧 Key Advantage

FULLY DETACHABLE
& MODULAR

All parts are detachable. Farmers can purchase only the attachments they need, making AgroTaskMaster highly affordable and customizable for every farm size and budget.

🔩 Detachable Parts 💰 Affordable 🎛 Customizable 📦 Modular Design
IoT Project
Companion Device

MEET
IRRIGO

Irrigo is our IoT-powered smart irrigation system. Using ESP8266 and Blynk IoT, it detects soil moisture in real-time and instantly notifies farmers when watering is needed — saving water and ensuring healthier crops.

Detects soil moisture level in real time
Sends instant notifications to farmers via mobile
Reduces water wastage significantly
Keeps crops optimally hydrated for better yield
Works independently alongside AgroTaskMaster
